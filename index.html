Tips

For Students

Try meatless

A Vegan/vegetarian lifestyle is the most sustainable diet as it stops the deforestation, soil degradation, and greenhouse gas emissions associated with meat production.

Reduce Packaging Consumption

Much of our waste is taken to landfill sites. Here, packaging materials including plastic, paper, tin and glass take hundreds of years to decompose. Try and go as package-free as possible and recycle wherever you can!

Waste less food

The average person wastes around 30% of the food they buy. Cut down on food waste as much as you can as this will also help with your student budget.

Eat seasonal, locally produced foods

They’re not only healthy and the most sustainable, but also the cheapest. Eating seasonally and locally can also reduce greenhouse gas emissions associated with food production, as well as CO₂ emissions resulting from transportation of food.
